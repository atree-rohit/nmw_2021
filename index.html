National Moth Week India, 2021 - Results

This page displays the results of National Moth Week India, 2021.

The data here is from a combination of observations of moths uploaded to iNaturalist and India Biodiversity Portal between 17-25 July 2021.

In total, 11,357 observations were uploaded by 710 contributors.

This page allows you to view the contributions filtered by :

  • no of observations / no of contributors
  • portal
  • Level to which the observation has been identified

By clicking on a state in the map, or in the table, you will see a list of all the observations from that state. Clicking on any observation will take you to the observation on the respective portal.

Observations from iNaturalist are highlighted in orange and from India Biodiveristy Portal are highlighted in blue

The data presented here was dowloaded on and 13 August, 2021 from India Biodiversity Portal and 18 August, 2021 from iNaturalist, and may have been updated on the respective portal since.
